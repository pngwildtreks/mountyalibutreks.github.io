What We Do

Facilitate The Climbing of the Mountain

Our tours are fully guided trekking experiences through the remote mountains and villages of Papua New Guinea. This is not luxury travel — expect rough terrain, changing weather, river crossings, basic village accommodation, and physically demanding hikes. Every expedition is led by experienced local guides familiar with the land and surrounding communities.

Includes Transportation, guided trekking experience, local landowner payment, accomodation, and on-ground expedition support.


Full Travel Package — USD $1500
Includes everything in the standard package plus return domestic airfare: Port Moresby → Mount Hagen Mount Hagen → Port Moresby


For travelers seeking real adventure, authentic culture, and the raw beauty of Papua New Guinea.
